Research

Help Advance Polygraph Science

Axciton Systems is committed to the ongoing scientific advancement of polygraph technology. We are currently conducting research and need your help.

Call for Submissions: Do you have confirmed DI (Deception Indicated) or NDI (No Deception Indicated) sessions? Sessions with confessed countermeasures or drugs? We want to hear from you.

Verified examination data — particularly cases where the ground truth outcome is confirmed — is invaluable for validating and improving polygraph algorithms and scoring methods. All submissions are treated with the highest level of confidentiality.

How to Participate

If you have confirmed examination sessions you are willing to contribute to our research database, please contact us. We will walk you through the process of securely submitting your data and explain how it will be used to benefit the profession.

Participants in our research program receive priority access to beta software releases and direct communication with our development team.
